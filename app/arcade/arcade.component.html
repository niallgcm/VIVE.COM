FOR VENUES

Experiences they'll love

Give customers access to an immersive world full of surprise with the VIVEPORT™ ARCADE Platform solution. This is a turnkey software platform providing a library of hundreds of VR titles approved for public use. It’s available worldwide to anybody running commercial locations such as arcades, internet cafes, theaters, shopping malls, and any other venue where VR entertainment can be offered to the public for a fee.

A complete solution

Viveport Arcade Platform provides both an operating system and a library of pre-negotiated content for commercial operators. We work directly with developers to secure their content and rights for distribution.

How it works

The download and registration process is done through a simple web portal. And fees are based on a pay-by-time model:

  • Purchase time-based points in advance
  • Use points to select from a library of VR titles
  • Retail prices to customers are determined at your discretion
  • Additional points are purchased at will
  • HTC handles payment to developers; no additional payment is required by you

FOR DEVELOPERS

Expand your reach

Engage people across the globe. As a developer, your VR creations will entertain people wherever they find a Viveport Arcade in a physical location. And you’ll generate a revenue stream for each published title.

Join a premium content lineup

More than 400 global developers have currently submitted their VR content through the Viveport platform. This content is up for consideration into the catalog for arcade operators.
